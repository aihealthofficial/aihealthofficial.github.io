Healthbot Privacy Policy

AIHealth Inc. ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. We follow key principles of the Personal Health Information Protection Act (PHIPA) in Ontario, Canada. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store the following types of information:

  • Personal Health Information (PHI): Symptoms, medical conditions, and health-related inquiries shared within the chatbot.
  • User Identifiers: Anonymized user IDs to associate conversations (no names, emails, or direct identifiers).
  • Technical Information: IP address, device type, and usage data for security and performance improvements.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Provide AI-powered health-related responses.
  • Enhance chatbot performance and improve user experience.
  • Maintain security and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

We do not use your PHI for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Protecting Your Information

We implement strong security measures to protect your data:

  • Data encryption (both in transit and at rest).
  • Strict access controlsâ€”only authorized personnel can view PHI.
  • Audit logs to track access and modifications for security.

Despite these efforts, no system is 100% secure.

4. Data Retention and User Rights

We retain collected data for service improvement and security.

  • Data is stored indefinitely unless internal policies change.
  • Users cannot request access, modification, or deletion of their chat history.
  • Users may stop using HealthBot at any time if they disagree with this policy.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share PHI. Data may only be disclosed under the following conditions:

  • To comply with legal or regulatory requirements.
  • To protect security and prevent fraud.
  • With trusted partners essential to delivering our services.

6. Third-Party Services

HealthBot may integrate with third-party service providers that meet industry security standards.

Note: We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Users are encouraged to review their policies.

7. Policy Updates

AIHealth may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users through our website or email if significant changes are made.
