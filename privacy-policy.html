Privacy Policy

At AIHealth, safeguarding your privacy is our utmost priority. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the measures we take to protect your information. By accessing our services, you agree to the terms described here.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless and personalized experience, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and other identifiers shared during registration or communication with us.
  • Health Information: Data from health assessments, symptoms, or other medical history shared through our tools.
  • Technical Information: Device type, IP address, browser details, and usage statistics for improving our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is utilized to:

  • Deliver tailored health insights and recommendations.
  • Enhance our AI algorithms and user experience.
  • Communicate updates, promotions, and service changes.
  • Maintain security and identify fraudulent activities.

3. Protecting Your Information

We employ robust security protocols to safeguard your data:

  • Advanced encryption for sensitive information.
  • Regular security audits to identify and mitigate risks.
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

4. Sharing Your Information

AIHealth does not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared only under these conditions:

  • With trusted partners essential to delivering our services.
  • When required by law or legal obligations.
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes.

Policy Updates

AIHealth may revise this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or regulations. We recommend reviewing this page periodically for the latest updates.
